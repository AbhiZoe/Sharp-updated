Privacy Policy

At Sharp Consulting, we are committed to protecting the privacy and security of our users' personal information. By accessing or using our website, you agree to the terms of this policy.

Sharp Consulting collects information through various means, including direct interactions with our website, such as form submissions, inquiries, or the use of cookies and similar technologies. Additionally, we may receive data from third-party sources or service providers to improve our services and enhance user experience. We prioritize transparency and lawful data collection practices while respecting your privacy rights.

Use of Information:

We utilize the data collected at Sharp Consulting to enhance our services, personalize user experience, analyze website usage patterns, and improve overall functionality. Your information may also be used for legal compliance, policy enforcement, or protection of our rights and interests. We handle your data with care and in accordance with this Privacy Policy and applicable laws.

Sharp Consulting implements robust security measures to safeguard your personal information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.
